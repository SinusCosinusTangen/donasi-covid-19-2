How to Donate

You will need to login first to make a donation. To donate through our website, simply fill in your identity in the forms available. Fill in the amount of money that you want to donate and choose your prefered payment method. Lastly, you can leave a personal message for those who are going to receive your donation. Click Donate and your donation has been recorded.

How to Give Testimonies

You will need to login first to make a donation. To submit a testimony, go to the Testimonies on the navbar, click "Give Us Testimonies", and fill in the forms available. To submit, you will have to click Submit.

How to Ask Questions

You will need to login first to ask a question. To ask a question, go to the Questions on the navbar, click "Ask", and fill in the box to ask the question. To submit, you will have to click Ask.

How to Register an Institution

You will need to login first to register an Institution. To register an Institution, go to the Institution on the navbar, click "Register Institutions", and fill in the box to ask the question. To submit, you will have to click Submit.

How to Report a Problem

You will need to login first to report a Problem. To report a problem, go to the Report on the navbar, click "Reports", and fill in the box to ask the question. To submit, you will have to click Submit.
